About Me

I build AI systems at the intersection of clinical medicine and machine learning — the kind that analyze medical images to detect conditions, classify audio signals to flag diseases, and process patient data to support real-time clinical decisions.

As an ML Engineer at CurieDx, a US healthcare startup, I work on production clinical AI pipelines deployed across multiple hospital sites. My work spans computer vision, generative AI, prompt engineering for diagnostic reasoning, and audio-based disease classification.

Beyond healthcare, I help businesses automate their operations — from intelligent data entry systems to automated quotation generation in supply chain workflows. I understand how businesses operate, identify bottlenecks, and build AI-powered automation that saves time and generates revenue.

I hold a B.E. in Biomedical Engineering and an M.Tech in AI & ML from Symbiosis Institute of Technology. I've published research in journals including SAGE and CMC, with international collaborators at University of Southampton NHS, Avignon Université, and Manipal Institute of Technology. I hold two patents and have 48+ citations on Google Scholar.

Based in Chennai, I'm available for speaking engagements, workshops, guest lectures, hackathon judging, and research collaborations.

What I Do

Areas of Expertise

Clinical AI Pipelines

End-to-end ML systems for healthcare diagnostics — from multi-site data ingestion to model deployment. Building multi-modal diagnostic pipelines combining imaging, symptom data, and audio signals for clinical decision support.

Medical Computer Vision

Object detection, image segmentation, and multimodal image classification for clinical applications. Training and deploying computer vision models on real medical imaging data across hospital networks.

Generative AI & Prompt Engineering

Designing clinical reasoning frameworks for medical LLMs. Systematic prompt engineering on models like MedGemma for diagnostic classification, plus GenAI R&D for healthcare and business applications.

Audio-Based Disease Classification

Building classifiers that detect disease conditions from audio signals. Ensemble models using focal loss and domain-adapted pretraining for production-grade diagnostic performance.

Business AI & Process Automation

Partnering with businesses to understand their operations and automate workflows end-to-end. Building intelligent data entry systems, automated quotation generation for supply chains, and custom automation pipelines that convert manual processes into revenue-generating machines.
